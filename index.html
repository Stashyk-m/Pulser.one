How It Works

From download to your first coached workout — under 5 minutes.

1

Scan your body

Open the app, stand in front of your phone camera, rotate slowly. In 60 seconds the movement engine maps your frame, posture, and proportions.

2

Get your personalised plan

The AI Coach builds a periodised programme based on your scan, goals, available equipment, and wearable data. No questionnaire — real data.

3

Train with live feedback

Prop your phone, start a session. The movement engine tracks every rep — form score, tempo, range of motion, coaching cues in real time.

4

Get smarter every session

Each workout feeds back into the engine. Your injury-risk score updates, your plan adapts, your progress compounds. The more you train, the better it coaches.
