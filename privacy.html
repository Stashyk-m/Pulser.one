3 Camera Data and On-Device Processing

The Pulser One camera feature processes your live video stream entirely on your device using the MediaPipe Pose Landmarker model. No video footage, raw frames, or image data is ever transmitted to our servers or any third party. The only data that leaves your device is the computed output — anonymised joint angle scores and rep counts — which may be stored in your account to power progress tracking.

This is by design, not by policy. The architecture makes camera data transmission technically impossible without your knowledge.

4 Health Connect and Apple HealthKit

With your explicit consent, we access health metrics from Android Health Connect or Apple HealthKit to compute your injury risk score and personalise your training programme. This data:

  • Is read only — we do not write to Health Connect without your explicit action
  • Is not sold to any third party, ever
  • Is not used for advertising purposes
  • Can be disconnected at any time in App Settings → Health
  • Is stored encrypted in your account and deleted permanently when you delete your account
  • Is processed in accordance with Google Health Connect data policies

5 Body Scan Data

Body scan results — including estimated body shape, frame size, proportions, and composition estimates — are derived from 2D camera keypoints and stored locally on your device. They are optionally synced to your encrypted account for progress comparison. These results are estimates based on validated anthropometric formulas and are clearly labelled as such within the app.

We do not share body scan data with any external party, advertiser, or insurer.

6 Waitlist and Email Communications

When you join the waitlist at one.pulser.fit, we store your email address and any optional information you provide (name, device platform, fitness goal, language, region) to send you updates about Pulser One's development and launch. We use formsubmit.co to process form submissions securely.

We will not share your email with third parties for marketing purposes. Maximum email frequency before launch: one per month. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or emailing go@pulser.fit.

7 Cookies and Tracking

The one.pulser.fit website uses only the following:

  • ipapi.co — a single anonymous API call to detect your country for regional pricing and language auto-detection. No persistent identifier is stored by us.
  • localStorage — stores your language preference locally in your browser only. No data is transmitted to any server.

We do not use advertising cookies, Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, cross-site tracking, or any analytics platform that identifies individuals. No cookie consent banner is required because we don't use tracking cookies.

9 Data Retention

  • Exercise history and body scan results: retained for the duration of your active account
  • Health data: retained until you disconnect Health Connect in settings or delete your account
  • Waitlist email addresses: retained until you unsubscribe or 24 months after collection, whichever comes first
  • Anonymised usage analytics: retained indefinitely (no personal identifier attached)
  • Account data: deleted within 30 days of a deletion request

10 Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format (JSON or CSV)
  • Restriction — request we limit processing of your data
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting prior processing

To exercise any right, email go@pulser.fit with subject "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In Poland: UODO — uodo.gov.pl. In other EU states, contact your national data protection authority.

11 Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, contact us immediately at go@pulser.fit and we will delete it within 72 hours.

12 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures including:

  • TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for health and body scan data at rest
  • Role-based access controls — staff access only what is necessary for their role
  • Regular security reviews and dependency audits
  • GDPR breach notification procedures (we notify within 72 hours if required)

13 International Transfers

Our primary infrastructure is hosted within the EU. Where any data transfer outside the EEA is required (e.g., for third-party services listed above), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with GDPR Chapter V, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where applicable.

14 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email (for waitlist members) or via an in-app notification, at least 30 days before changes take effect. The updated Policy will always be posted at one.pulser.fit/privacy with a revised effective date.
