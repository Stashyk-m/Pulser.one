5 Body Composition Estimates

The 360° body scan uses visual anthropometric analysis — proportions derived from 2D camera keypoints — to estimate body shape, frame size, and body composition. These are estimates based on validated anthropometric formulas (including WHO/Deurenberg indices) and are clearly labelled as estimates within the app at all times.

These results are not equivalent to DEXA scan, bioelectrical impedance, hydrostatic weighing, or clinical measurement, and should not be used to inform medical decisions. The purpose is motivational progress tracking, not clinical measurement.
