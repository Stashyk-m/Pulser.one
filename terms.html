Body Composition Estimates

The 360° body scan uses visual anthropometric analysis (proportions derived from 2D camera keypoints) to estimate body shape, frame size, and body composition. These are estimates based on validated anthropometric formulas (WHO/Deurenberg) and are not equivalent to DEXA scan, bioelectrical impedance, or clinical measurement. All results are labelled as estimates within the app.
